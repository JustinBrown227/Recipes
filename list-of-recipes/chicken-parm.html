Chicken Parmesan

Description

Chicken Parmesan is a classic Italian-American dish that features a juicy, breaded chicken cutlet coated in golden breadcrumbs, fried to perfection, and topped with a rich marinara sauce. The dish is finished with a generous layer of melted mozzarella and Parmesan cheeses, then baked until bubbly and golden. Served over a bed of al dente spaghetti or alongside a fresh salad, it is a comforting and flavorful meal with a perfect balance of crispy, savory, and cheesy goodness.
